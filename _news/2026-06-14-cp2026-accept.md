title: Two papers are accepted at CP 2026
---

I co-authored two papers accepted in CP 2026: my paper “On Inferring Cumulative Constraints” about improving CP scheduling with deriving hidden resource constraints using lifting, and the paper “Formally Verified Certification of Constraint Programming Proofs,” co-authored with [Maarten Flippo](https://maartenflippo.com/), [Tip ten Brink](https://tiptenbrink.nl/), [Clément Pit-Claudel](https://pit-claudel.fr/clement/), and [Emir Demirović](https://emirdemirovic.com). For the former, you may be interested to read [an accompanying post]({{ '/blog/2026/cp2026-lifting/' | relative_url }}) for a more accessible overview of the ideas I introduce.
